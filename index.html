- About Us -

Our founder and CEO of Spa Castle had a vision to create a single retreat made of tradition, culture, luxury, and recreation for everyone to enjoy, all year round. Spa Castle is the translation of his dream into a reality shared by many. The elegant combination of blissful spa services and treatments, exclusive state-of-the-art aqua therapy facilities and saunas, and the plethora of traditional health offerings from around the world make for a truly extraordinary experience for our customers.

- The Experience -

Body Treatments & Beauty Spa

Indoor & Outdoor Bade Pools

Spa Castle offers a plethora of services as unique and diverse as the needs of our clientele. From Hand and Foot Reflexology in a plush leather recliner, to a Golden Caviar Facial in our Beauty Spa, there's a treatment or massage fit for anyone's pampering needs.

Our Bade Pools are filled with strategically placed aqua-therapy jets, aimed to massage different areas of the body. Not only are our clients relaxing in our pools, but they're also receiving acupressure hydrotherapy that enhances circulation, relieves muscle tension, and more.



Locker Room & Bath Area

Sauna Valley

After receiving one of our fresh spa uniforms to change into, visitors will be directed, by gender, to a grand locker and bath area. Here they will find separate shoe and clothing lockers, a vanity and lounge area, as well as an ornate traditional “bath-house” area.

Sauna Valley offers a truly breathtaking experience. Equally ornate in aesthetics and structure, each sauna is made of authentic materials that are known for their medicinal powers through the combination of heat and the natural essence of the room itself.
